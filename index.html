What I help with

Product Development Strategy

I'll architect the full solution at a nuts-and-bolts level — defining the interfaces early so your team can parallelize work and make the most of the time and people you have to get to market as quickly as possible. We'll tune the plan to your stage together, whether you're preparing to deploy your first systems or scaling up the fleet.

Firmware & SDK

I'll own the firmware layer and deliver an SDK for your custom hardware. That includes pinning down RTOS requirements, hardware selection, and end-to-end testing. I have particular experience with high data throughput and imaging systems, though the work spans a broad range of embedded targets.

Firmware Debugging & Root Cause Analysis

For teams stuck on a hard bug. Crashes, race conditions, memory corruption, hardware-software interaction failures — I find what's actually wrong and explain why. Available as a standalone engagement when you need an outside expert who can go all the way down the stack.

IndustrialNext Embedded Vision Systems
